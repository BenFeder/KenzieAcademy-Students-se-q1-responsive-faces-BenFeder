Our Story

Today, our company is a family-owned business operating 21 store location across Indiana. Each store offers a range of eye health services that help patients correct and protect their vision. In addition, our stores carry a large selection of designer frames and fashion-forward eyewear fro Women, Men, and Kids.

More than 75 years later, we've helped Indiana residents maintain quality vision and find their perfect pair of eyewear throughout all our stores.
